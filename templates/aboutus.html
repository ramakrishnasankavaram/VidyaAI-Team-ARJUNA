About VidyaAI++

VidyaAI++ is a next-generation educational platform designed to empower students from Below Poverty Line (BPL) government schools. Our vision is to make high-quality, personalized learning accessible to every child, regardless of background or language.

What We Do

  • Deliver AI-powered tutoring and mentorship in multiple languages
  • Enable students to learn in their native tongue
  • Connect learners with caring mentors
  • Track progress and boost confidence
  • Make learning fun with gamification and emotion tracking

Our Mission

We strive to bridge the educational gap for underprivileged students by combining technology, innovation, and compassion. VidyaAI++ is built by passionate learners, for learners—driven by the belief that every child deserves a chance to shine.
